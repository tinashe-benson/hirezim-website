The positioning

You Are Not Everyone.

One day, everyone will have an AI agent. A personal one. A consumer one. The same one your kid uses to plan vacations and your assistant uses to draft replies.

You're the founder. The CEO. The operator. You have revenue, employees, clients, contracts, and a brand on the line. Your AI can't be a consumer toy with your name on it. It has to be governed, context-aware, voice-aligned, and accountable to the way you actually run your business.
